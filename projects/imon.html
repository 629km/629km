Overview

‘imon’ is a ride-on IV rack made for children with better usability, safety, and functionality. Considering the special needs of child patients, the seat position can be adjusted. Anti-twist and anti-trap functions for tubes are added to prevent safety accidents. A bag rack, cellphone cradle, and whiteboard are also provided along with detachable equipment for oxygen tanks, electronic devices, etc. and attachments for medical components. Unlike the uniform appearance of existing IV racks, imon’s exterior has a cute, familiar shape and uses colors, materials, and finishing materials for additional comfort and more safety.
