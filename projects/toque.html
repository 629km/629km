TOQUE — Interactive Pass Box

Interactive pass box for disaster-response medical wards — guides safe hand-over of supplies via airflow control, interlock, and UI cues to minimize cross-contamination.

Overview

TOQUE is the new concept for a Covid-19 quarantine ward pass box. Existing pass boxes offer only one-way passage to deliver necessary supplies things into the quarantine ward from outside. TOQUE is a unique design solution, creating a two-way passage with UV sterilization and communication functions to aid communication between medical staff and patients in and outside the quarantine ward. Medical staff are able to check on patients without wearing level-D protective equipment. Covid-19 patients in quarantine face less anxiety from being in an isolated room because they are offered direct care via TOQUE.
