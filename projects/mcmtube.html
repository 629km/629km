Overview

MCM TUBE is a modular medical ward system designed for fast deployment during infectious disease outbreaks. It combines an inflatable air-tent with prefabricated pressure-controlled panels, allowing installation in under 15 minutes with a small team. Clean air enters from the top, flows downward, and contaminated air is extracted through HEPA-filtered negative pressure circulation, ensuring bio-safety for patients and staff. Its modular structure expands from a single isolation bed to a full field hospital, adaptable to indoor gyms, parking lots, or outdoor plazas. By separating movement paths of patients, staff, and waste, MCM TUBE supports efficient medical workflows while reducing patient anxiety.
