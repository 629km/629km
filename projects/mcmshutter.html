Overview

MCM SHUTTER is an infectious water treatment device that is connected to a temporary toilet in a pop-up clinic or a mobile negative pressure ward. Especially, it is possible to install on the existing toilet facility in order to change the general hospital to infectious disease hospital. It is possible to build infectious management toilet environment not only time saving aspect but also cost effectiveness.
