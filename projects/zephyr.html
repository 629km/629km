Zephyr — Wound Care for Visual Impairment

A portable device that assists visually impaired individuals in self-care. Tactile guidance and a hygienic design enable safe and consistent treatment.

Overview

Zephyr is an at-home wound treatment device especially designed for visually impaired people. Their hands, arms or legs can be easily cut, burn or scratched because they often use their fingers, hands or limbs to find something at home. It is not easy to detect the exact location of cut or burn and also the cut or burn could be a secondary infection due to using their finger to find the location. Zephyr can easily detect the location of the wound with wind blown from the device and conveniently cure the wound with the roler type sticking plaster. Visual impaired people can avoid possible secondary infection as well.
