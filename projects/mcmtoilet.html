MCM Toilet System

Toilet system for disaster-response medical tents. Modular negative-pressure toilet unit that separates staff/patient flows and prevents aerosol contamination.

Overview

MCM TOILET is a modular toilet that can be assembled and used in a pop-up medical facility. It consists of a toilet space with a negative pressure device and an independent water tank. The design of MCM TOILET was considered so that the air and water of infected patients can be safely managed. The built-in pump is used where there is no inclination of the floor. The size and structure were decided in consideration of the convenience of installation and use. MCM TOILET could be used in connection with various infrastructures. It can be easily connected to an existing pipe or a toilet equipped with an automatic sewage discharge system. In addition, it can be used without piping connection by using autonomous carts for transferring clean or wastewater.
